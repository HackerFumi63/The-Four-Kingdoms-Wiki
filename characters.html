Characters

Naofumi Iwatani

The main character of Four Kingdoms and one of the founding kingdom owners of Oasis. (Kingdom Owner of Imperial Hyrule)

Yukinoshita Itati

One of the main characters of the Four Kingdoms series who is the king of neo 4K and the leader of the Iwatani Revengers.

Dimitri Terogami

A major supporting character of Four Kingdoms who is one of the Iwatani Revengers from Nebula that helped Oasis.

Charlie Davidson

One of the major supporting characters of Four Kingdoms and main character of Charlie Mafia. (Kingdom Owner of Farmtopia)

Lilyana Enderwolfie

A major supporting character and one of the main founding kingdom owners of Oasis. (Kingdom Owner of Lunanovia)

Nicholas Pikaman

Previously the leader of the hellshire gang, he is one of the major supporting characters of Four Kingdoms as well as the main character in his spinoff story, The Hellshire Gang. (Kingdom Owner of Grave of Glenville)

Mateo Davidson

Charlie Davidson’s younger brother and 4K government official.

Andrew Benison

A 4K high ranking official whose power runs high in the judicial system.

Benjamin Benison

Younger brother of Andrew and one of the squad leaders of the Four Kingdoms military.

Christopher Catman

One of the citizens of Neutral City who revolted against the government and inspired the rise of the Iwatanists.

Carter

One of Nicholas' friends who won the presidential election for temporary kingdom owner of Imperial Hyrule.

Evan

One of the citizens of Neutral City who later joined the Iwatanists or clan Iwatani and their base at Iwatani island.

Larakawa

One of the citizens in the countryside of oasis (outer oasis), she lived in alvinville and later moved to lunanovia.

Eren Kruger

The leader of the llama mafia,he is the main antagonist of part 1 of Four Kingdoms and the main character of his spinoff story, El Llama Mafia.

Bakura

One of the main hackers of the Llama Mafia who is known for his invisibility powers.

Neatswing

An iconic member of the Llama Mafia known for his humour and charismatic personality.

Nicola Richard

Naofumi’s old best friend who is the protagonist in his spinoff: N&N: Brotherhood.

Destiny

The princess of Melromarc who joined the Llama Mafia.

Hitch

One of the Llama Mafia members who can change their form.

Flotch

One of the llama mafia members who is siblings with Hitch.

Farias Edelguard

Leader of the Tophats and antagonist of season 5 of Four Kingdoms.

Adam Alvin

One of the original kingdom owners, he is one of the antagonists of Four Kingdoms The Final Season part 1 and was a part of the Tophats.

Jocelyne Jones

One of the original founding kingdom owners, she is a talented SCP specimen user as well as one of the first.

Nirvana Anastatia

The main leader of Nebula as its main symbol of the country’s fuhrer.

General Cody

One of the generals of the Nebulan military who mentored Rocco, Elsa, and Dimitri.

Rocco

One of the military men of Nebula and friend of Dimitri Terogami.

Elsa

Dimitri Terogrami’s old childhood friend and crush.

Aaron Davis

A mysterious man from Nebula who was well known for his SCP research and powers.

Kyrie Pax

A mysterious girl who works under Jake Walker as his right hand spy.

Jake Walker

Owner of the famous Los Sephora business, he is the infamous kingpin of the underground and face of the people.

John Walker

Jake’s younger brother who is as ruthless and cunning as Charlie is intelligent and tactical.

Big Dog

One of the most feared sub henchmen that work under Jake Walker’s el cartel.

Hanzo Grey

One of the smartest citizens in Oasis who was responsible for helping both Naofumi and Charlie defeat their enemies.

Zaki

The main supplier of operation 11 and one of the main members of the Charlie Mafia.

Ysmael Gonzales

One of the main members of the llama mafia who Charlie cherishes like a younger brother.

Thomas Baldwin

The main CEO of Jake’s laundering business.

Michael Bridges

A mysterious therapist who Charlie meets in his house to discuss the past.

Claude Goodman

An infamous lawyer known for his crime works in Los Sephora and Neutral City.

Julia

One of the mattresses of the Orphanage for the Worthy.

Captail Jeral

Leader of the Oceania Mafia.
